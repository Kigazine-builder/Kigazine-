Before you share

Kigazine is for creative ideas, stories, clubs, reviews, and learning. Posts should not include private information or anything mean, scary, unsafe, or grown-up.

  • Use a nickname, not your full real name.
  • Do not post your address, school, phone, email, passwords, or exact location.
  • Ask a trusted grown-up before sharing anything online.
